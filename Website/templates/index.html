An Agent Based Model simulation
on information diffusion

By Mads Malling Petersen & Martin Højer Larsen

Lets see how an influencer might influence a normal person like you and me

The gray circle is the average person in a social network. The blue square represents an influencer pushing factually based news. The read triangle on the other hand, is an influencer prone to share fake news.

Try to press the influencers to see how they influence their (small) network

It seems that the influencers are not very good at their job. Keep in mind however, that this was only one try and in a very small and isolated network.
In the real world, you're connected to many people - and many influencers.
