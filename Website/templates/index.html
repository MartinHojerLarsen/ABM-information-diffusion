An Agent Based Model simulation
on information diffusion

By Mads Malling Petersen & Martin Højer Larsen

Lets see how an influencer might influence a normal person like you and me

It seems that the influencer is not very good at his job. Keep in mind however, that this was only one try.
In the real world, you're connected to many people.

Story time!

So as you can see, the stronger the bond between people, the more likely they are to believe what they recieve from their peers.
